About our parish

St Francis of Assisi Catholic Church, Buoho — a community of prayer in the Diocese of Konongo-Mampong.

Our parish is named after St Francis of Assisi, the saint of humility, peace, and love for God’s creation. In Buoho we strive to live that same spirit: welcoming strangers, caring for the vulnerable, and rejoicing in the Gospel.

Buoho lies in the Ashanti Region of Ghana. The wider Catholic life of this area is closely linked with the Diocese of Konongo-Mampong, and nearby St Mary’s Sanctuary (Buoho Grotto) remains a cherished place of pilgrimage for the faithful.

As a parish family we celebrate the Sacraments, form disciples, and serve our neighbourhood in Christian charity.

Our mission

To know Christ, to worship God in spirit and truth, and to serve Buoho with Franciscan joy.

Worship Celebrate the Eucharist and the sacramental life of the Church
Form Nurture children, youth, and adults in Catholic faith and practice
Serve Extend hospitality, mercy, and practical care to our neighbours
