Faith rooted in Buoho

We welcome every person seeking Christ — families, youth, pilgrims, and visitors to the Buoho community.

St Francis of Assisi Catholic Church stands as a living parish community in Buoho, within the Catholic Diocese of Konongo-Mampong. Inspired by St Francis, we seek simplicity, joy, and care for one another.

Here the Word is proclaimed, the Sacraments are celebrated, and the faithful are sent into daily life as witnesses of peace.
